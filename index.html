About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor in Computer Science at IMT (Ecoles Mines-Télécom) Nord Europe. I a member of the Autonomous Resilient Systems group (ARTS), part of the Digital Sciences CERI (Center for Education, Research and Innovation) under the supervision of Professor Sylvain LECOMTE, Director.

From June 2022 to February 2024, I was a post-doct in Computer Engineering with the FUN Team, Inria Lille-Nord Europe under the supervision of Nathalie Mitton, Research Director.

I am also a temporary teacher in Computer Science at the University Institute of Technology and the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science of the University of Ngaoundéré, Cameroon.

On July 2021, I defended my Ph.D. thesis under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Anna Förster and Profs. Blaise Omer Yenke/Paul Dayang from Bremen and Ngaoundéré universities respectively. My Ph.D. works focused on a wireless underground sensor network for precision agriculture.

Apart from my technical skills, I describe myself as a cheerful person who is pleasant to work with. I have a passion for new technologies and I like the work well done.
